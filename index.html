New to the SCA?

Welcome! The Society for Creative Anachronism (SCA) is an international organization dedicated to researching and re-creating the arts and skills of pre-17th-century Europe. We study and practice medieval and Renaissance arts, crafts, combat, and culture.

If you're interested in joining us or learning more about what we do, please contact our Chatelaine (New Member Liaison):

Lord Ahikaga Tatsukage (James White)
